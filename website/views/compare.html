About our use of http

Here at quantified dev we care deeply about keeping your data safe. While we're in beta, in lean startup style, we've deferred building https support so that we can prioritise figuring out the features you're interested in. We realise sending our tokens over http means they can be intercepted by baddies. At the moment no personally identifyable information is sent to quantified dev. Those baddies can't tell whose data they are looking at. We would rather focus on building great features for you - learning what you want - and build https support before we exit beta.

If you are concerned about our approach and your data, please discontinue using quantified dev. Better yet, tell us we're wrong by emailing us at team@quantifieddev.org
